Builder-Prep

The unofficially superior guide to preparing for the DigitalCrafts immersive bootcamp!

What's your prep level?

Welcome!

You’ve made a great decision to have enrolled in the 16-week DigitalCrafts coding bootcamp! The skills you’ll learn during the program are invaluable to your career as a software developer.


As current students in the program, we’ve compiled a list of resources for you to help prepare you for the rigorous curriculum ahead. You can never be too prepared entering the program!


While the assigned pre-work is sufficient, our goal is to provide the resources necessary to ensure you excel during the program. For that, we’ve established ‘Core’ pre-work, as well as a list of ‘Additional Resources’ submitted by users of this site.


Aim to complete the core requirements first, then move on to the additional resources page where user submitted content can be voted on based on relevance and helpfulness. Additionally, you can filter through the material to brush up on a particular language or technology.

Beginner

  • No prior experience? No problem! Start with the beginner resources and you'll be on your way to starting your career in web development.

Intermediate

  • Have you dabbled with some online tutorials? Maybe you can solve some basic functions in JavaScript or other functional coding language. Skip the beginner resources and jump straight in to the intermediate resources.

Advanced

Advanced students should know who they are. They may have studied computer science, or have built basic web applications. We reccomend they go straight to the advanced topics to fill any gaps they might have.
